# Vision Summary

SkyNode is a web platform for planning short flight-based trips in Europe.

## Target Users

- Students and young adults planning short private trips.
- Users who want a fast trip draft in one place.
- Users who need clear flight options and activity ideas without professional booking-platform complexity.

## High-Priority Features

- Flight search by origin, destination, and date.
- Flight results with price, duration, stops, and times.
- Filtering and sorting of flight results.
- AI-generated daily itinerary based on destination, duration, budget, and interests.
- Saving prepared trips.

## Medium-Priority Features

- Manual itinerary editing.
- Regenerating individual itinerary days.
- Approximate daily cost overview.
- Destination attractions and points of interest.
- Chat interface for itinerary adjustments.

## Low-Priority Features

- Public share links.
- Map visualization and optional OpenSky flight traffic layer.

## Explicit Non-Goals

- Booking or buying flight tickets.
- Hotel reservations.
- Offline support.
- Train, bus, or non-flight transport support.
- Detailed tracking of one concrete flight as the core feature.
